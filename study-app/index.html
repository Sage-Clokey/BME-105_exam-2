Formulas & Quantitative Reference

Every calculation on Exam 2 in one place.

Hardy-Weinberg Equilibrium

p² + 2pq + q² = 1
p + q = 1
p=freq(A), q=freq(a). p²=AA, 2pq=Aa, q²=aa.
5 assumptions: random mating · no mutation · no migration · no selection · infinite N

Chi-Squared Test (HWE)

χ² = Σ (O − E)² / E
df = genotype classes − 1 (= 1 for biallelic). χ² > critical value → reject HWE. E = HWE expected freq × N.

Recombination Frequency

RF = recombinants / total offspring
1 cM ≈ 1% recombination
Unlinked loci → RF = 50%. RF limits fine-scale mapping resolution.

Relative Fitness & Selection

W = 1 − s
w̄ = p²W_AA + 2pqW_Aa + q²W_aa
s = selection coefficient (0=neutral, 1=lethal). Normalize best genotype to W=1. w̄ = mean pop fitness.

Allele Freq Change (Selection)

Δq ≈ −sq²(1−q) / w̄
Per-generation change for recessive allele. As q→0, most copies in heterozygotes → very slow purging → exponential decay of q².

dN/dS Ratio

dN/dS < 1 → purifying
dN/dS = 1 → neutral
dN/dS > 1 → positive selection
dN = non-synonymous rate. dS = synonymous (neutral baseline) rate.

Complementation Test

m₁/+ × m₂/+
Mutant F1 → same gene (fail to complement). WT F1 → different genes (complement). Both mutations must be recessive and in trans.

Bisulfite Sequencing

unmethylated C → U → reads as T
methylated C → stays C
Compare bisulfite-treated to reference to find 5mC at single-base resolution. Nanopore/PacBio detect methylation directly from signal.

CRISPR Guide RNA

crRNA: ~20 nt + PAM (NGG)
PAM = NGG immediately 3' of protospacer on non-target strand. Cas9 cuts ~3 bp upstream. NHEJ→indels→KO. HDR+template→knockin.

GWAS Threshold

p < 5 × 10⁻⁸
Bonferroni for ~1M independent SNP tests. Hit SNP may not be causal — it's in LD with causal variant in same haplotype block.

Genetic Drift

Effect ∝ 1 / N_e
Small N_e → large drift → fast fixation/loss. Founder effect = new small population. Bottleneck = temporary N reduction.

Molecular Clock

divergence time = mutations / rate
Neutral mutations accumulate at ~constant rate. Calibrated with fossils. Gene tree ≠ species tree (ILS, HGT, gene duplication).
